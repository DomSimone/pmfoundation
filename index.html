Introduction

As a manifestation of 15 years of traceable work in community engagement and global advocacy; The Patson Malisa Foundation is an Organisation designed for the successful implementation of Citizen-oriented and Citizen-Led projects with regards to sustainable economic and social development in the continent of Africa and beyond.

Cognizant of the development visions at national, continental and global platforms with regards to the inclusion of Citizen voices in the global development agenda as is displayed in the adopted Development plans, the Foundation embarks on the responsibility of bridging the gap between the diplomatic process and delivery of the Sustainable Development Agenda to Citizens in Africa and the globe at large.

We are mindful of Africa’s Agenda 2063, which instructs in Aspiration 6 that efforts of public and private entities are to strive to produce an Africa where development is people-driven, unleashing the potential of its women and youth. Hence the rallying motto for a people-centred and people-driven Africa.

We acknowledge the growing influence of the African continent on the world stage. Its impact on the Post-2015 Development Agenda’s status cannot be ignored. It is therefore our mission to ensure the full engagement of the continent in the realization of goals of development as set in the Post-2015 Development Agenda of the United Nations.

Africa, having experienced over 60 years of defining its democratic and inclusive society, needs as many vehicles as possible for its Citizens to harness the opportunities ahead for the strengthening of the core values instilled by the fathers of our young continent. We believe that our Foundation can be such a vehicle of this order, and thus it is in our mandate to assemble and strengthen the necessary public and private platforms infrastructure for the achievement of national and continental development.
