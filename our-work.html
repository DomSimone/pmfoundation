Our Work On The Ground

The Foundation engages with local and international organizations, both public and private, in executing the following functions:

1. Establishment, implementation and management of Sustainable Development Projects in the African continent as a whole.

2. Diplomatic representation and interventions related to local and national government liaison for implementation of Citizen-led Sustainable Development projects in the continent.

3. Facilitation of Contact Programs & Forums in the African Continent for citizen capacity development and mobilization.

4. Investment in the development and preservation of the Arts & Culture of the African Continent

In addition to the bilateral and multilateral work executed, the Foundation serves as the official document library, media archive and official channel of public communication for Patson Malisa.
